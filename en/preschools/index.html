Prion at preschools


Easy school communication and documentation, from one simple app.

Prion makes it easy for teachers to give parents a daily insight to preschool activities while documenting the children’s development. With the app teachers can save and share content with parents while keeping the children’s integrity intact. Prion is available across platforms, both as an app for iPhone/iPad, Android and on the web.


The app is easy to use, for anyone

The app has a friendly interface and is fast and easy to use for anyone, even with less technical skills. It only takes a few seconds for teachers to select a few photos or videos, write a caption and make it available for their selected target.


A digital portfolio, for every child

Every child has their own portfolio where teacher can save material related to that child only. The childrens individual portfolios can only be accessed by the teacher and its guardians. The guardians can choose to receive a push notification to their phone when there’s new content in the portfolio.
